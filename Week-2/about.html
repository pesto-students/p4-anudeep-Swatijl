What I do

So yeah, I am a software developer by profession, so I do coding all day. That sounds boring, right? Let me make it more interesting for you to read. If someone comes to me & ask any of these question -

  • How can I expand my business?
  • How can I manage employees data working in my organization?
  • How to make my local shop more popular among different regions?
  • How can I get a date for next valentine's?

All these problems & many more have one solution i.e. a software. There's nothing in this world that cannot be resolved through a software. That's another reason I chose software industry.

How I do it

Let's come to - how I will develop a web application for you. So, my approach is to start with understanding the problem statement very well. I will ask you a lot of questions before actual development, so bear with me. Once the requirement is clear, I start developing the application. I take suggestions for the look & feel if you have any, otherwise I use my approach (just google some good templates & add its html/css with few changes). I am good at backend, so no worries in that part. I mainly focuse on how I can make the application more secure (oh yeah, you cannot mess up with the security part). And the tech stack I work on - Js frameworks(frontend), Django(backend) & some Databases.

Why I do it

I develop software because I love coding & through my skill, I can solve real-world problems. And yeah, money is also an important factor. I am really getting paid well for what I do.
